CriticalCommoditiesMacro

Hormuz still gated as Iran demands Tehran-designated routes, keeping a geopolitical tax on tankers even as ceasefire holds.

Iran issued a fresh warning last Thursday that vessels must follow Tehran-designated corridors through the Strait of Hormuz, a fortnight after IRGC strikes on the Ever Lovely and Kiku triggered a four-day US–Iran exchange of fire. One-fifth of global oil and LNG transits the choke point, and traffic has not returned to pre-war levels despite the June 17 MOU signing.

Market Impact
Keeps a $3–5 risk premium in Brent even as OPEC+ adds 188 kbpd in August; tanker rates and insurance still elevated. Bid under gold at $4,149; supportive for defense complex and USD funding demand.
Watch: BZ, CL, LMT, RTX, XOM, GLD, XLE.

Dow prints record above 53,000 as AI trade re-accelerates on Hon Hai revenue and Nvidia roadmap reassurance.

WatchCommodities

Brent slides below $72, four-month low, as OPEC+ approves 188 kbpd August hike and Saudi cuts Asian OSPs.

Brent traded near $71.85 — its lowest since late February — after OPEC+ signed off on an 188 kbpd August quota increase, continuing the progressive unwind of production curbs. Saudi Aramco cut Arab Light for Asian buyers by $1.10 to a $1.50 discount to the regional benchmark, a soft signal from the swing producer.

Market Impact
Softer crude is a tailwind for US CPI print and a headwind for energy earnings; XLE/SPX ratio has rolled from June highs. Refining margins still constructive on distillate. Weaker oil consistent with the recent DXY slip to 100.95 that tightens the global monetary loop.
Watch: XOM, CVX, XLE, USO, XOP.
Source · Trading Economics — Brent crude

Bitcoin breaks $62K after $4.5B June ETF outflow — worst month on record — despite a brief early-July inflow reversal.

BTC opened at $63,590 then slipped to $61,678 intraday, with ETH tracking lower to $1,737. Spot BTC ETF flows recorded $4.5B of net outflows in June — the worst month since launch — before a $221.7M single-day intake on July 2 ended a ten-day, $2.7B outflow streak.

Market Impact
Institutional demand cooling reads as a risk-taking rethink, not a regime shift; miners under real pressure. Cross-asset: crypto beta traders often anticipate small-cap and secondary tech drawdowns — worth watching alongside the semi wobble.
